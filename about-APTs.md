Sawa Abubakar 🌿 — hizi **resources** ulizopewa ni muhimu sana kwa kujifunza zaidi kuhusu **threat actors na motivations zao**:  

- **APT Groups and Operations**  
  - Ni juhudi ya wataalamu wa usalama kuorodhesha **Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) groups** na shughuli zao.  
  - Ilianza mwaka 2015 na bado inaendelea kusasishwa na security community.  

- **APT Groups**  
  - Hapa unaweza kupata maelezo ya makundi tofauti ya APT, tactics zao, na maeneo wanayolenga.  

- **How Microsoft names Threat Actors**  
  - Microsoft imebadilisha mfumo wa majina ya threat actors na sasa inatumia **theme ya hali ya hewa (weather)**.  
  - Mfano: Storm, Blizzard, Typhoon — kila jina linaonyesha tabia au origin ya actor husika.
